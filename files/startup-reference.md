# DepMi — Startup & Business Reference

> Compiled: Feb 28, 2026. Not legal or financial advice — treat as informed startup common sense.
> Consult a Nigerian lawyer/accountant for formal decisions.

---

## 1. Registration Timing

**Domain**: Register it now. Cheap (~$10–15/yr) and protects your brand.
- Recommended registrar: Namecheap
- Priority: `depmi.com` first, `.ng` optional and can wait

**CAC**: You don't need it to *build*, but you need it before you can:
- Give equity to anyone
- Open a business bank account
- Sign contracts with partners/vendors
- Onboard investors

Register CAC before bringing on any co-founder who asks for equity.

---

## 2. Co-Founders

Having 2–3 co-founders improves investor perception — investors back *teams*, not solo founders.
But only bring on people who fill real skill gaps:
- Tech / CTO
- Operations / Business Development
- Growth / Marketing

**Rules:**
- Co-founder conflicts are a top 3 startup killer — choose wisely
- All co-founder equity must vest over **4 years with a 1-year cliff**
  - Meaning: if someone leaves before month 12, they get 0%
  - After month 12, they earn equity monthly for the remaining 3 years
- This protects you if someone walks away early

---

## 3. CAC Registration — Which Type

Register as a **Private Limited Company (Ltd)**, not a Business Name.

| Type | Best For |
|---|---|
| Business Name | Sole traders, freelancers |
| Private Ltd (Ltd) | Startups, equity, investors |

A Private Ltd gives you a shareholder structure, limited liability, and a clean cap table — all required for investment.

---

## 4. Company Structure: DepMi vs. MiTE

**Recommendation for now**: Register **DepMi Ltd** directly. Keep it simple.

The holding company structure (MiTE Holdings → DepMi Ltd) makes sense *later* when you:
- Launch a second product
- Want to sell DepMi while keeping other ventures
- Need to ring-fence liability between companies

Adding that layer now will confuse investors and slow down registration.
Build the holding structure after your first funding round or when you have a second product.

The goal of "sell DepMi, keep MiTE" is valid and fully achievable — just do it in Year 2–3, not Year 0.

## 6. Friends & Family Round — Equity Rules

**For ₦300K–500K: Don't give equity. Use a loan instead.**

Equity at this amount creates cap table mess that will annoy real investors later.

### Option 1 — Simple Loan (Cleanest)
Write a one-page agreement: repay principal + 20% flat fee when DepMi raises its first round or within 24 months. No equity, no legal complexity.

### Option 2 — SAFE Note
"I'll give you equity at whatever valuation my first investor sets, at a 20% discount."
No fixed percentage now, but they benefit when real money comes in. Investor-friendly.

### Option 3 — Equity (Last Resort)
Only if they insist. Keep total friends/family equity under **5%** across everyone.
For ₦300K–500K specifically: 1–2% is fair.

> **Rule of thumb**: The less money you raise, the less equity you should give.
> A VC giving $200K for 15% is standard. Your cousin giving ₦200K for 15% is a cap table disaster.

## 7. Investment Ask (Pre-Seed)

At MVP stage (working product, no revenue yet):

| Stage | Typical Ask | Equity |
|---|---|---|
| Pre-seed (idea + MVP) | $50K–$200K | 10–20% |
| Seed (early traction) | $300K–$1M | 15–25% |

**For DepMi right now:**
- **Ask**: $100K–$150K USD (~₦150M–₦225M)
- **Equity offered**: 10–15%
- **Use of funds**: 18-month runway — office, salaries, hosting, minimal ads

**On royalties vs equity**: Royalties are rare in tech and signal low conviction. Use equity.
For pre-seed, consider a **SAFE note** to avoid fixing valuation before you have real traction data.

**Golden rule**: Retain >50% after your first funding round.
If an investor wants >20–25% at pre-seed, that's a red flag.

## 9. Simple Loan Agreement Template

```
SIMPLE LOAN AGREEMENT

Date: _______________

PARTIES
Borrower: Manuel [Your Last Name]
          Building DepMi (a social commerce platform)
          [Your address]

Lender:   [Lender's Full Name]
          [Lender's address]

LOAN DETAILS
Principal Amount: ₦ _______________
Purpose: Business registration, domain, and operational
         costs for DepMi

REPAYMENT TERMS
The Borrower agrees to repay the full principal plus a
flat goodwill fee of 20% (total: ₦ _______________)
under whichever of the following happens FIRST:

  (a) DepMi raises its first external investment round, OR
  (b) 24 months from the date of this agreement

If neither has occurred by the 24-month mark, both
parties agree to renegotiate in good faith.

NO EQUITY
This loan does not entitle the Lender to any shares,
ownership stake, or decision-making authority in DepMi
or any related company.

GOVERNING LAW
This agreement is governed by the laws of the Federal
Republic of Nigeria.

SIGNATURES

Borrower: _________________________ Date: __________
          Manuel [Last Name]

Lender:   _________________________ Date: __________
          [Lender's Full Name]

Witness:  _________________________ Date: __________
          [Witness Full Name]
```

### How to Use the Template
- Print two copies — one for you, one for the lender
- Get a witness — someone not involved signs too (neighbor, colleague)
- WhatsApp confirmation — after signing, send them a photo of the signed copy and save the chat. In Nigeria, this is often more useful than paper in a dispute
- 20% flat fee — simple and clearly defined; adjust if lender wants more/less
- 24-month backstop — protects you from being pressured to repay before you're ready

> If the amount is above ₦500K or the relationship is complicated, spend ₦20,000–₦50,000 to have a lawyer draft a proper agreement.

## 10. Priority Order Right Now

1. Register the domain (today, if not done)
2. Register **DepMi Ltd** at CAC (before onboarding any co-founder asking for equity)
3. Finish the MVP (OTP, invite, deps system)
4. Get 10–50 beta users and document early traction
5. *Then* approach investors with a working product + real user numbers

> An investor meeting now would be premature. Even 20 active sellers on the platform is worth more
> in a pitch deck than a polished MVP with zero users. Traction data is your strongest leverage.
