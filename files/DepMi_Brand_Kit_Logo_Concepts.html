Payment Gateway Comparison: Is There Cheaper Than Paystack?

Here's the honest answer: OPay, PalmPay, Moniepoint, and Paga are NOT payment gateway APIs — they're mobile money operators and POS providers. They don't offer the marketplace split payment API that DepMi needs. The real comparison is between these:

OPay, PalmPay, Moniepoint, Paga
These are mobile wallets / POS providers, NOT online payment gateway APIs. They don't offer marketplace split payments, subaccounts, or checkout APIs for your app. They can be FUNDING SOURCES (users pay from their OPay/PalmPay wallet), but they can't replace Paystack/Flutterwave as your payment processor.

💰 My Recommendation: Start with Flutterwave

Flutterwave is 0.1% cheaper than Paystack (1.4% vs 1.5%) and the fee is capped at ₦2,000. On a ₦10,000 transaction, you save ₦10. That's small — but at scale (1M transactions/year), it's ₦10M in savings.

More importantly, Flutterwave supports 30+ African countries out of the box, which aligns with DepMi's pan-African expansion plan. Paystack only covers Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya, and South Africa.

The smartest approach: Use Flutterwave as your primary gateway and integrate Paystack as a backup/alternative. Many Nigerian platforms run both simultaneously. If Flutterwave has downtime, payments fail over to Paystack automatically. This also lets you A/B test which has better success rates for your specific user base.

For accepting OPay/PalmPay as payment METHODS (so users can pay from their wallets), both Flutterwave and Paystack support this through bank transfer — when a user pays via bank transfer, they can use their OPay or PalmPay app to complete the transfer. No extra integration needed.
