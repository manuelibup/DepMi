In consideration of the mutual promises and obligations set out in this agreement — including the equity promise in Section 3 and the services to be rendered by Lead Engineer — and intending to be legally bound, the parties agree as follows:

Definitions

The following definitions apply throughout this agreement. Additional defined terms are defined where they first appear in the body of the agreement.

  1. “Operational” means the DepMi platform is publicly accessible and has recorded at least one user login or transaction in the preceding 30 days;
  2. “Active Development Period” means any week in which the Founder has assigned pending work to Lead Engineer;
  3. “Material Breach” has the meaning given in Section 12.1;
  4. “Gross Misconduct” and “Dishonesty” have the meanings given in Section 11.3;
  5. “Pre-existing IP” has the meaning given in Section 4.

1. Role & Responsibilities

Lead Engineer agrees to serve as Lead Engineer of DepMi. Responsibilities include:

  1. Identifying and resolving existing technical debt as prioritized by the Founder;
  2. Developing and deploying new product features as directed by the Founder;
  3. Maintaining platform stability and uptime above 99% over any rolling 30-day period;
  4. Dedicating a minimum of _______ hours per week to DepMi during active development periods, defined as any week in which the Founder has assigned pending work. Any reduction below this minimum during an active development period must be agreed in writing with the Founder in advance. During periods where the Founder has not assigned any pending work, the minimum hours obligation is suspended — but Lead Engineer remains on reasonable standby and must respond to urgent technical matters within 24 hours of notification. The Founder may not invoke unavailability during an unassigned period as grounds for termination or breach. Consistent unavailability during active development periods constitutes grounds for termination for cause. Hours and availability are tracked through weekly written progress updates to the Founder or any other method mutually agreed in writing. GitHub commit volume alone is not conclusive evidence of hours worked; the Founder may request evidence of meaningful work completed at any time, and consistent inability to demonstrate meaningful progress during an active development period constitutes grounds for termination for cause;
  5. Reporting directly to the Founder on all technical matters;
  6. Protecting all system credentials, API keys, environment variables, and access tokens in their possession and never sharing them with any third party without prior written approval from the Founder;
  7. Reporting any security incident, breach, or vulnerability to the Founder immediately upon discovery;
  8. Handing over all credentials, repositories, admin access, and technical documentation to the Founder immediately upon request or termination.

2. Compensation

Lead Engineer receives no salary, retainer, commission, bonus, or payment of any kind.

The sole compensation for services rendered under this agreement is the equity promise in Section 3. Lead Engineer enters this agreement voluntarily with full understanding of these terms and waives any future claim to monetary compensation for work performed under this agreement.

3. Equity

Subject to CAC incorporation of DepMi as DEPMI LIMITED, a Private Limited Company, and Lead Engineer not having breached any clause of this agreement, Lead Engineer is promised _______% of the total issued share capital of DEPMI LIMITED at the time of formal share issuance. This percentage is calculated against total issued shares at the moment of issuance and is not derived from or limited by any option pool.

3.1 Vesting

  1. Total vesting period: 4 years;
  2. Cliff: 1 year from the Active Work Start Date written at the top of this agreement — not the signing date;
  3. After the cliff: remaining equity vests monthly in equal portions over the following 36 months;
  4. Vesting requires Lead Engineer to remain actively engaged and not in breach at any point. For purposes of vesting, “actively engaged” means consistently meeting the minimum hours commitment in Section 1(d) and not having received a valid termination notice. If the Active Work Start Date is not filled in at the time of signing, it defaults to the Date of Signing.

3.2 Dilution

Lead Engineer’s equity is subject to proportional dilution from future investment rounds, option pool expansions, or new share issuances. _______% means _______% of shares at time of issuance — not a guaranteed permanent percentage across all future funding rounds. Lead Engineer accepts this condition by signing this agreement.

3.3 Eligibility

Lead Engineer remains eligible for equity so long as they have not:

  1. Resigned voluntarily;
  2. Been terminated for cause;
  3. Breached any clause of this agreement.

3.4 Timing & Right of First Refusal

This equity promise is not legally binding as a share transfer until formal share issuance following CAC incorporation. Both parties agree to formalize this into a shareholder agreement immediately upon incorporation, which shall include the following Right of First Refusal:

Before selling, transferring, pledging, charging, encumbering, using as collateral, or otherwise disposing of any equity — in whole or in part — Lead Engineer must first offer it to the Founder in writing at the same price and terms offered by any third party. Any attempt to pledge or charge equity as loan collateral without following this process is void and of no effect. The Founder has 21 days to accept or decline in writing. If the Founder declines, the Founder may nominate an alternative buyer within a further 21 days. If no alternative buyer is nominated or completes the purchase within that period, Lead Engineer may approach a third-party buyer, but any such sale requires the Founder’s prior written approval. The Founder may withhold approval at their sole discretion. No transfer of equity is valid without following this process in full.

The shareholder agreement to be executed upon incorporation shall comply with the Companies and Allied Matters Act (CAMA) 2020, which shall govern the rights and obligations of all shareholders.

4. Intellectual Property

All work product created by Lead Engineer for DepMi — including but not limited to code, technical architecture, documentation, scripts, and tooling — belongs entirely to DepMi and its Founder, whether created before or after the date of this agreement.

Lead Engineer waives any claim to ownership or compensation for such work beyond what is stated in this agreement and confirms that no work product delivered infringes any third party’s intellectual property.

All code and software created for or contributed to DepMi constitutes a copyrightable work under the Copyright Act (Cap C28 LFN 2004). The IP assignment in this section transfers all such copyright to DepMi and its Founder with immediate effect.

If Lead Engineer incorporates any code, libraries, tools, or materials created prior to or outside of this engagement (“Pre-existing IP”) into DepMi’s codebase or platform, Lead Engineer must disclose this to the Founder in writing within 48 hours of incorporating it. By incorporating Pre-existing IP into DepMi’s platform, Lead Engineer grants DepMi and its Founder a perpetual, irrevocable, royalty-free licence to use, modify, and distribute that Pre-existing IP as part of the DepMi platform. If Lead Engineer is unable or unwilling to grant such a licence for any Pre-existing IP, they must not incorporate it without the Founder’s prior written consent. Failure to disclose Pre-existing IP constitutes a material breach.

5. Confidentiality

5.1 Protected Information

Lead Engineer agrees not to share, disclose, transmit, or publish the following without prior written permission from the Founder:

  1. Monetization model, fee structure, and pricing strategy;
  2. Any non-public performance data including registered users, daily active users (DAU), monthly active users (MAU), seller count, buyer count, transaction volume, Gross Merchandise Value (GMV), revenue figures, growth rate, retention rate, churn rate, and conversion rate;
  3. Product roadmap and planned features;
  4. Business partnerships, negotiations, and investor discussions;
  5. User, seller, or buyer personal data of any kind;
  6. API keys, credentials, access tokens, environment variables, and security architecture;
  7. Internal communications and strategic decisions.

This applies to information Lead Engineer directly observes, infers, is told verbally, or accesses through any tool — not only written information.

5.2 What Is Not Confidential

  1. DepMi’s technology stack (Next.js, Prisma, Neon, Vercel, Cloudinary);
  2. DepMi’s publicly stated mission and public product features;
  3. Information already publicly available through no fault of Lead Engineer.

5.3 Duration

  1. General information: 2 years after termination;
  2. User personal data, credentials, and security architecture: indefinite, with no expiry date.

5.4 Disclosure Under Duress

If Lead Engineer is compelled to disclose confidential information through genuine threat, coercion, or physical force, Lead Engineer is not liable for that specific disclosure, provided that:

  1. Lead Engineer notifies the Founder in writing within 48 hours of the incident; AND
  2. Lead Engineer cooperates fully with any police report or legal investigation.

Failure to report within 48 hours removes this defense entirely. All other confidentiality obligations remain in full effect.

6. Data Protection

Lead Engineer agrees to handle all user, seller, and buyer personal data strictly in accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and any successor legislation. Lead Engineer shall not access, process, store, or transfer personal data beyond what is strictly necessary to perform their duties under this agreement, and shall not retain any personal data after termination.

Lead Engineer further acknowledges that unauthorized access to, or misuse of, DepMi’s computer systems, user data, or credentials may constitute a criminal offence under the Cybercrimes (Prohibition, Prevention, etc.) Act 2015, and that DepMi reserves the right to report any such conduct to the appropriate authorities without prior notice to Lead Engineer.

7. Conflict of Interest

Lead Engineer discloses that they ARE currently involved in another venture: ___________________________.

Lead Engineer confirms this venture does not directly compete with DepMi and agrees to:

  1. Inform the Founder immediately in writing if this changes;
  2. Not use DepMi’s resources, data, or confidential information to benefit any other venture;
  3. Not use DepMi’s codebase as the foundation for any other product.

8. Non-Compete

During this agreement and for 36 months after termination — or the maximum period a court of competent jurisdiction finds enforceable, whichever is shorter — Lead Engineer agrees not to:

  1. Found, co-found, join, advise, invest in, or provide technical services to any platform or product that directly competes with DepMi’s core business, defined as any platform that combines social features (including but not limited to feeds, profiles, following, comments, or reviews) with commerce or marketplace functionality, and targets buyers, sellers, or merchants in Africa or the African diaspora — regardless of primary positioning, stated niche, or geographic registration;
  2. This restriction applies regardless of where Lead Engineer or the competing platform is physically located. A platform targeting African buyers or sellers from any country in the world falls within this restriction.

Lead Engineer may seek written consent from the Founder to engage in activities that may fall within this clause. Consent shall not be unreasonably withheld.

9. Non-Solicitation

Lead Engineer agrees not to directly or indirectly solicit, recruit, or encourage any DepMi user, seller, buyer, employee, contractor, or advisor to leave or reduce engagement with DepMi — for any purpose including joining a competing platform — during this agreement and for 24 months after termination, regardless of which party initiates termination.

“Indirectly” includes acting through a third party or sharing DepMi’s internal data with a competing platform.

10. Non-Disparagement

Both parties agree not to make disparaging, defamatory, or damaging statements about each other — publicly or privately — during or after termination of this agreement. This includes statements on social media, to investors, to the press, or to potential partners.

Nothing in this section prevents either party from: (a) making truthful, factual statements in legal proceedings, regulatory filings, or arbitration; (b) providing honest references or disclosures in good faith to investors, professional advisors, or potential partners who are under a confidentiality obligation; or (c) reporting a criminal act or regulatory violation to the appropriate authorities. Only false or malicious statements are restricted by this clause.

11. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with 14 days written notice per Section 14 (Notices). Upon either party giving or receiving notice of termination, the Founder may immediately suspend Lead Engineer’s access to all DepMi systems, repositories, platforms, and credentials as a precautionary measure, without this constituting termination for cause or triggering any additional rights for Lead Engineer. All of Lead Engineer’s obligations under this agreement — including confidentiality, non-compete, IP assignment, and data protection — remain fully in effect throughout the notice period. Lead Engineer shall not download, copy, export, or transmit any DepMi code, data, credentials, or confidential information during the notice period.

11.1 Lead Engineer Resigns Before Cliff

All equity is void. No equity is retained. No compensation of any kind is owed.

11.2 Lead Engineer Resigns After Cliff

Only equity vested to the date of resignation is retained. All unvested equity returns to the Founder immediately. No accelerated vesting under any circumstance.

11.3 Terminated for Cause

Founder may terminate immediately without notice for breach of any clause, gross misconduct, or dishonesty. For purposes of this agreement, “gross misconduct” means: theft or fraud involving DepMi assets or funds; deliberate sabotage of DepMi’s technical infrastructure or codebase; unauthorized transfer or sale of DepMi assets, data, or credentials; disclosure of confidential information or credentials to a competitor or third party; or any criminal act committed in connection with Lead Engineer’s role at DepMi. “Dishonesty” means knowingly providing false representations to the Founder about material matters, including work completed, system access held, or conflicts of interest. All equity — including vested equity — is forfeited in full and returns to the Founder upon a finding of gross misconduct or dishonesty as defined above.

11.4 Terminated Without Cause Before Cliff

If Founder terminates Lead Engineer without cause before the 1-year cliff date, Lead Engineer is entitled to no equity. The cliff applies regardless of which party initiates termination.

11.5 Terminated Without Cause After Cliff

Lead Engineer retains only equity vested to the date of termination. All unvested equity returns to the Founder immediately.

11.6 Obligations on All Terminations

Lead Engineer must within 48 hours:

  1. Transfer full admin access of all repositories, platforms, services, and accounts to the Founder, including all systems listed in Schedule B to this agreement and any additional systems accessed during the engagement. Handover is only considered complete when Founder has confirmed receipt of access to every item listed in Schedule B;
  2. Permanently delete all local copies of credentials, codebase, and data;
  3. Confirm deletion in writing to the Founder;
  4. Cease representing themselves as affiliated with DepMi in any capacity.

11.7 Constructive Dismissal

If the Founder ceases all communication with Lead Engineer for more than 30 consecutive days without written explanation, or unilaterally revokes Lead Engineer’s access to all DepMi systems without cause or notice, Lead Engineer may serve written notice of constructive termination to the Founder. Lead Engineer must first notify the Founder in writing and allow a further 14 days for the Founder to restore communication or access before invoking this section. If constructive termination is validly invoked, Lead Engineer shall be treated as having been terminated without cause under Section 11.4 or 11.5 as applicable.

12. Breach & Remedies

12.1 Equity Forfeiture

A “material breach” includes but is not limited to: any violation of Sections 5 (Confidentiality), 7 (Conflict of Interest), 8 (Non-Compete), or 9 (Non-Solicitation); failure to transfer access upon termination; gross misconduct; or dishonesty as defined in Section 11.3. Material breach of Sections 4, 5, 6, 7, 8, or 9 results in immediate, full forfeiture of all equity — vested and unvested.

12.2 Dissolution Before Incorporation

If DepMi ceases operations prior to CAC incorporation for any reason, all equity promises under this agreement are void and no compensation of any kind is owed to Lead Engineer. Lead Engineer acknowledges this risk and accepts it by signing this agreement.

12.3 Liquidated Damages

Lead Engineer agrees that a breach of the confidentiality, non-compete, or non-solicitation clauses would cause harm to DepMi that is difficult to quantify precisely. Lead Engineer therefore agrees to pay ₦2,000,000 per proven breach as a reasonable genuine pre-estimate of loss, not as a penalty. Multiple breaches result in multiple charges.

12.4 Injunctive Relief

Founder may seek an emergency court injunction to stop any ongoing breach without first proving financial loss.

12.5 Legal Costs

The prevailing party in any dispute arising from this agreement shall be entitled to recover their reasonable legal fees and arbitration costs in full from the non-prevailing party. A party that initiates a claim and withdraws it before a final decision shall be treated as the non-prevailing party for purposes of this section.

13. Representations & Warranties

Lead Engineer represents and warrants that:

  1. They have full legal capacity to enter this agreement and are not a minor;
  2. They are not currently bound by any employment contract, non-compete, IP assignment clause, or any other agreement that would conflict with or restrict their ability to perform services for DepMi;
  3. No work product delivered to DepMi will be subject to ownership claims by any current or former employer, client, or institution;
  4. If currently employed elsewhere, they have confirmed that working with DepMi does not violate their employment terms. DepMi and its Founder bear no liability if this representation proves false.

14. Incorporation Commitment

The Founder commits to pursuing CAC incorporation of DEPMI LIMITED within 6 months of this agreement, provided the business remains operational. If incorporation is delayed beyond this period for reasons within the Founder’s control, Lead Engineer may request a written update and revised timeline, which the Founder shall provide within 14 days of such request.

If incorporation has not occurred within 9 months of the Date of Signing and the business remains operational, Lead Engineer may give 30 days written notice of intent to terminate this agreement on the basis of incorporation delay, provided that Lead Engineer has already made at least one written update request per the paragraph above and at least 30 days have elapsed from that request without a satisfactory response. If termination is invoked under this paragraph, Lead Engineer shall retain all equity vested to the date that the 30-day notice expires, and all unvested equity returns to the Founder. This right is the Lead Engineer’s sole remedy for incorporation delay and does not entitle Lead Engineer to any monetary compensation.

15. Succession

Lead Engineer’s equity promise and all rights under this agreement are personal and non-transferable. They do not pass to Lead Engineer’s heirs, estate, legal successors, assigns, or any company Lead Engineer owns or controls. If Lead Engineer dies or becomes permanently incapacitated before formal share issuance, all equity promises under this agreement are void and return to the Founder. If the Founder dies or becomes permanently incapacitated, all obligations under this agreement transfer to the Founder’s legal successors, who shall be bound by its terms including the equity promise to the Lead Engineer.

13D. Breach Cure Period

In the event of a breach that is capable of remedy, the non-breaching party shall give the breaching party 7 days written notice to remedy the breach before enforcing the consequences in Section 12. This cure period does not apply to:

  1. Disclosure of confidential information or credentials;
  2. Gross misconduct or dishonesty;
  3. Active violation of the non-compete or non-solicitation clauses.

These categories trigger immediate enforcement with no cure period.

15. Dispute Resolution

Both parties agree to attempt genuine negotiation for a minimum of 14 days before pursuing any legal action. If unresolved after 14 days, disputes shall be referred to a mutually agreed neutral mediator. If mediation fails, disputes shall be resolved by arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act of Nigeria. If both parties cannot agree on an arbitrator within 14 days of mediation failure, either party may apply to the Lagos Court of Arbitration (LCA) to appoint one. The arbitration shall take place in Lagos State unless both parties agree otherwise in writing.

16. Severability

If any clause of this agreement is found invalid or unenforceable by a court, that clause alone is severed or reduced to the minimum extent necessary. The remainder of the agreement continues in full force and effect.

17. Company Name & Continuity

This agreement refers to the business currently trading as DepMi, intended to be incorporated as DEPMI LIMITED with the CAC. If the intended registered name is unavailable or changed for any reason prior to or following incorporation, this agreement remains fully valid and binding on both parties. A change in the company’s trading name, registered name, or legal structure does not affect the obligations, equity promises, or any other terms of this agreement. All references to “DepMi” in this agreement shall be read as referring to the business and its legal successor entity, regardless of name.

18. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria.
